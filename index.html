📜 How to Play Chess

A guide for brave young knights! 🗡️

🎯 The Goal

👑
Trap the enemy King so it cannot escape. That is called Checkmate — you WIN!

🖱️ How to Move a Piece

1️⃣
Click any of your pieces. It glows yellow to show it is selected!
🟢
Green dots appear — click one to move there.
🔴
Red ring = you can capture that enemy! Click it to take them!
🔄
White always goes first. Then players take turns one at a time.

⚠️ Special Things to Know

🚨
Check! Your King is in danger — the square flashes red. You MUST move out of danger immediately!
🏰
Castling: The King and Rook can swap if neither has moved yet — great for hiding the King!
👑
Pawn Promotion: When a pawn reaches the far end, you get to pick a new powerful piece!
⚖️
Stalemate: If a player has no moves but is NOT in check — it is a draw. No winner!
